If you want to learn origami you have come to the right place!


As you might have guessed I am honey and I run an origami studio. On this website, I display my work
and findings as well as some walkthroughs on making things with origami. Origami is a japanese art form
where one folds paper often square, too achieve an article of art, and it can be anything! A simplistic plane
or a complex sculpture!
