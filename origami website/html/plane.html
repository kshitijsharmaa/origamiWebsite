Paper airplane tutorial

Things you will need:

  • A4 paper
  • Your hands

First, fold your paper in half, length wise like so.

Using the fold line as a guide take the two corners of the page and match them
together at the center (fold line) now take the triangle that has been made and
fold them down. Now, we do the same thing as before, we take the corners and match them
together at the center using the fold line as a guide. Our plane is almost finished.

Here is how it should look so far:

Now, fold them in half and make the sides opposite of your face touch.
You can fold the wings and your plane is done!.
